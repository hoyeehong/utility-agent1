NEW WATER TARIFF STRUCTURE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Component 1: Water Usage Charge
  Formula: usage (m³) × $1.43 per m³
  Example: 8.3 m³ × $1.43 = $11.87

Component 2: Waterborne Tax
  Formula: usage (m³) × $1.09 per m³
  Example: 8.3 m³ × $1.09 = $9.05

Component 3: Water Conservation Tax
  Formula: water_usage_charge × 50%
  Example: $11.87 × 0.5 = $5.94

Total Water: Component 1 + Component 2 + Component 3
Example: $11.87 + $9.05 + $5.94 = $26.86 ✅
